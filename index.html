Frequently Asked Questions

The subject I want isn't listed. Can you still help?

We offer a huge range of subjects and we try and find tutors from all backgrounds to help with your specific needs. If you have a particular subject in mind, please get in touch and we'll do our best to assist you. We can even help with non-traditional subjects if you're looking for something specific.

How are your lessons conducted?

Our lessons are conducted online, and all sessions are recorded for safeguarding purposes. We use a dynamic whiteboard technology that allows for interactive learning, making it easy to share resources and collaborate in real-time.

Eventually, we plan to implement a system that allows students to access their lesson recordings and resources through a dedicated portal. This is still currently under development.

What payment options are available?

We offer flexible and secure payment options, including easy-to-use payment structures via Stripe and Klarna.

Additionally, we provide options for installment payments to make our services more accessible.

Some of our tutors will also offer a discounted price for block bookings or ten or more lessons, though this is down to their discretion

How do I get a free trial lesson?

Some of our tutors will offer you a free trial lesson upon request. This is a great way to see if their teaching style aligns with your learning needs.

We encourage all of our tutors to offer their first lesson for free, but this is their decision and we cannot enforce this upon them, as their time is valuable

What if my child needs more than just academic support?

We focus on more than just grades. Our tutors are dedicated to building academic confidence, fostering a genuine enjoyment of the subject, and supporting your child's mental well-being.

How do I become a tutor with TutorBloom?

If you're passionate about teaching and want to make a difference in students' lives, we'd love to hear from you! Please fill out our tutor application form, and a member of our team will get in touch to arrange an interview, which will include a discussion about your teaching philosophy and approach, as well as a demonstration of your teaching skills.

Do you offer group tutoring sessions?

Yes, we offer both one-to-one and group tutoring sessions. Group sessions can be a more affordable option and also provide opportunities for collaborative learning among peers.

What are the freelance content creator opportunities?

We are always looking for talented freelance content creators to join our team. Opportunities include managing social media posts, writing blog posts, and creating educational materials. This is a great way to gain experience and build your portfolio while working with a supportive team. This is also a role where you can earn commission, based on your contributions, not only boosting your income but also enhancing your professional growth.
